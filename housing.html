Finding a Rental in the Netherlands
with a Furry Friend

As a pet owner in the Netherlands, finding a rental property that welcomes your beloved dog can be a daunting task. With the country facing a housing shortage and landlords often preferring tenants without pets, navigating the rental market can feel like an uphill battle. However, with the right approach and a few helpful strategies, you can increase your chances of securing a pet-friendly rental for you and your canine companion.

Start Early

One of the keys to finding a rental property that accepts pets is to start your search early and be persistent. Begin looking for suitable accommodations well in advance of your desired move-in date, as this will give you more time to explore different options and increase your chances of finding a pet-friendly landlord.

Utilize Online Resources

Take advantage of online rental platforms and websites that allow you to filter search results based on pet-friendly criteria. Websites like Funda, Pararius, and Kamernet often have filters specifically for pet-friendly properties. Additionally, consider joining social media groups or online forums dedicated to pet owners in the Netherlands, as members may share valuable insights and listings.

Prepare a Pet Resume

To demonstrate that you are a responsible pet owner, consider preparing a pet resume to accompany your rental application. Include information such as your dog's breed, size, temperament, vaccination records, and references from previous landlords or neighbors who can vouch for your pet's behavior.

Provide Proof of Training

If your dog has undergone obedience training or earned certification as a therapy or service animal, be sure to provide documentation to the landlord. Demonstrating that your dog is well-trained and well-behaved can help alleviate any concerns the landlord may have about renting to a pet owner.

Finding a pet-friendly rental in the Netherlands can be challenging, but with patience, persistence, and a proactive approach, it is possible to secure suitable accommodations for you and your furry friend. By following these tips and tricks, you can navigate the rental market with confidence and increase your chances of finding the perfect home for both you and your beloved dog.
