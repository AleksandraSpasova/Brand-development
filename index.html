Who are we

“In a world where emotional and practical support is rare to find away from home, we believe everyone deserves assistance in tough moments, because success is only built through teamwork.”

“We as Doggify fight to make a drastic difference and reshape the idea of foreign big dog ownership, making it not just barely manageable but truly rewarding.”

Community - We aim to create a safe space where international dog owners can not only bond over their pets but, above all, find a sense of support and belonging, and make long-lasting friendships.

Shared Responsibility - We encourage you to actively contribute to the well-being of the community and its members. Doggify centers on collective action by offering dog care assistance, including sitting and walking, and sharing knowledge and resources through our blog.

Empathy - We understand cultural shock, and we have experienced the same challenges that you’re currently facing as an expat in The Netherlands. And there are many like us. Doggify is a platform that inspires us all to connect and sympathize with each other. Our approach is to address your various needs with genuine care, ensuring a welcoming experience filled with understanding and warmth.

Cultural Inclusivity - While Dutch organizations often use language and policies that isolate internationals, at Doggify, we thrive in an international environment. Our platform welcomes all Dutch residents, regardless of background or origin, and deeply values your diverse perspectives.
