What we offer

Daily pet sitting

This is a feature designed specifically for those who have a packed schedule. We at Doggify respect all your hard work, and we want to help you by offering daily visits by trusted dog-sitters. Prepare your dog to say goodbye to separation anxiety and welcome a lifestyle full of games, cuddles, and tasty meals!

Daily walking

Daily dog walks are the highlight of your pup's day. With Doggify, they can become even better! Whether in a single stroll or a group tour, your dog will return home ready for a warm bath, and with many new furry friends made along the way.

Daily visits

If your dog wants to take it slow, we are here to offer daily check-ins. A friendly caregiver will stop by your home 3 times a day to feed, play with, and give attention to your dog, ensuring they're content until you return. The best part - you will no longer worry you forgot to turn off the oven!

Overnight sitting

Everyone deserves to go to a party once in a while. When your friend is getting married or that trip to Ibiza is finally making it out of the group chat, choose Doggify! A caring sitter will stay in your home, making sure your dog feels safe and loved while you're away.
